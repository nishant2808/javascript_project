MOOSE
Moose are large, majestic deer found in northern forests. Known for their impressive antlers, males grow broad, flat racks each year. They are strong swimmers and feed on aquatic plants, shrubs, and trees. Moose are solitary animals, thriving in cold climates and playing a key role in their ecosystem.

ELEPHANT
Elephants are large, intelligent mammals known for their distinctive trunks, tusks, and massive ears. They are social animals that live in herds and are native to Africa and Asia. Elephants play a vital role in ecosystems by shaping their habitats and are cherished for their gentle yet powerful nature.

LEPARD
Lepards are agile, spotted big cats found in Africa and parts of Asia. Known for their stealth and climbing ability, they often drag prey into trees for safety. Leopards adapt to diverse habitats, from savannas to forests, and their golden coat with black rosettes provides excellent camouflage in the wild.
